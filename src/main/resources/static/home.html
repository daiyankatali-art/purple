Good to Know Before You Book

  • Booking window: You can only book a trip for today or up to 7 days ahead. Trips further out aren't open yet - check back closer to your travel date.
  • Limited seats: Each route has a limited number of seats per day. Once a trip is fully booked, that exact route and date won't be available until another day.
  • One ticket per trip: Submitting the same booking twice won't create a duplicate - you'll just see your existing ticket.
  • Two-step booking: Submitting the form reserves your seat as Pending. Your booking isn't confirmed until you complete payment on your ticket page.
  • Keep your ticket: After payment, print or save your ticket and present it when boarding.
  • Fares vary by route: Prices are calculated automatically based on your selected cities and number of tickets.
  • Arival and Departure time:Arrival and departure time for all travellers is 8:15AM and 9:00AM respectively
